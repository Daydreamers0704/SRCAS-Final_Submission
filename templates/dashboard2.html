Diseases Plant of Classification

Plant diseases can be broadly classified according to the nature of their primary causal agent, either infectious or noninfectious. Infectious plant diseases are caused by a pathogenic organism such as a fungus, bacterium, mycoplasma, virus, viroid, nematode, or parasitic flowering plant.

  • Based on the Host plant affected
  • Based on plant part affected.
  • Based on Pathogen Generation.

We identify the plant disease by simply uploading the the picture of the infected plant parts and display the name and variety of the plant or crop disease . We give the recommandation to come away from the problem.

E-Commerence Platform for Farmer

E-Farming commercial website is a online shopping website where buyer can buy farm produce directly from farmers and every farmer can able to post their own product in website directly. Various types of farmer's products are available for purchase at reliable price.They can easily Buy and Sell their product via the platform for the reliable price.

The main objective of this platform is to help the farmers from indian villages to sell their product to different city product easily with reliable price and quality. This platform will act as the unique and secure way to perform agro-Marketing and purchasing.The site will guide the farmer`s in all aspects,the current marget rate of different products ,the total scale and earned profit.
